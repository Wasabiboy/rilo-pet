Rilo Pet

A small companion that opens rilo.studio — and pings you when a Rilo task finishes.

"Auto" is safest. Switch to "Bridge only" once the Rilo team ships the companion event hooks. Switch to "DOM only" if the bridge misbehaves.

Fires only when you're on another tab/app. The pet bubble always appears when you're in-browser.

Off by default. Requires chime.mp3 in the extension folder.

Detection runs on rilo.studio tabs only. The pet hides itself while you're on Rilo.
